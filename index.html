About US

Our idea of luxury is to spend time with people whose company we cherish.

For more than a decade BUCCARA – in Swahili it means Secret Place – has been hosting getaways for a select group of friends and acquaintances at our own castles, villas & yachts. And because the concept has been so well received, BUCCARA wants to open its doors to a select group of new partners.
